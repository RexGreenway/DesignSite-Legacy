Realities Checked

Logo Design. The research team behind 'Realities Checked: Interpreting Stories of Crime In Gypsy And Traveller Communities' study the experiences of crime and justice for Gypsy and Travellers in England. I was commissioned to develop and realise a logo to represent their group and was given agency to interpret the goals of their research. The logo can be seen in use on the group's website; https://www.realities-checked.org/.
